  1. DYNAMICAL ANALYSIS OF THE TRANSMISSION OF COVID-19 PANDEMIC

    Undergraduate project work
    Co-authors: Dr. Md. Rifat Hasan (Supervisor)
    Keywords: COVID-19 , SEIR Model, Epidemic Modeling

    In our project, we analyzed COVID-19 transmission using the SEIR model. We reviewed relevant literature, formulated the model mathematically, and proved key properties like stability and positivity. We employed numerical simulation methods, specifically MATLAB, to solve the differential equations using techniques such as Runge-Kutta methods. Using real data from Bangladesh, India, Malaysia, and Brazil, we generated simulations to compare predictions with actual cases. This helped us understand differences in disease dynamics across these countries. Finally, we highlighted how the model can inform public health strategies and suggested directions for future research.
